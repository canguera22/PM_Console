IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

* The NTIS DMF file is delivered monthly via secure transfer.
* File sizes may exceed several million records.
* Initial historical load must be handled separately from monthly incremental checks.
* Monthly checks must NOT reprocess historical data.
* Execution timing must align with business operations and reporting cycles.
* Reports must be available to operations and compliance teams.

NON-GOALS

* Real-time eligibility checks are out of scope.
* Manual review workflows are handled outside this system.
